RECALL

Product Recall

Ed Dawn

(6/26/2017)

The following products tested positive with salmonella. Although very few products tested positive, Ed Dawn has decided to recall all products that were purchased between 06/01/2017 and 06/17/2017

  • Romaine Lettuce (#000456)
  • Tomatoes (#565784)
  • Celery Stick (#333897)
  • Cucumbers (#763994)

If any of the products you purchased from Ed Dawn is among the list above, please contact Ed Dawn's recall hotline:

(800)-654-3999
